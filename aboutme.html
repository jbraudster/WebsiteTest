Hello!  My name is Kenny Schichtl.

I am a student at Arkansas Tech University working on getting a bachelor's degree in Cyber Security.

I have a fairly eclectic taste in games.  When I was four years old, I was playing Super Smash Bros. on the Nintendo 64. I grew up with a SNES, Playstation 2, and an N64.  I started PC gaming shortly before the era of the Xbox, Playstation 3, and Gamecube.  I began playing games on pc like Warcraft II, and Diablo. I ended up getting a decent computer by pulling funds from my scholarship money for college which, let's face it, was the only choice I had. I also succumbed to League of Legends my freshman year.  Towards the beginning of my Military career, Final Fantasy XIV became my life. Finally, I got into Indie games as a result of what my close friends inspired me to play.
